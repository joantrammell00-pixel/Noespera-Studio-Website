Full Practice Suite

Organizational, Brand & Growth, and People & Performance Practice

Our Practice Areas

Drawing on 30+ years of strategic leadership experience across Gallup, L'Oréal, Johnson & Johnson, Pierre Fabre, and Sandoz Biopharmaceuticals, these offerings address the full arc of organizational and brand development.

Organizational Practice

  • Organizational Meaning Science™
    Proprietary research discipline examining how meaning is created, interpreted, and carried—or lost—across teams, leadership layers, and brand touchpoints. Grounded in ITES™ (Interpretation Through Experience Scale, patent pending), a measurement methodology that surfaces how meaning is being interpreted across organizational contexts.
  • Leadership Development
    Executive coaching and training focused on sensemaking, decision architecture, and meaning stewardship for leaders navigating growth, transition, or cultural realignment.
  • Board Advisory
    Governance support for boards and fiduciary leadership navigating strategic change, mission alignment, or organizational transition.
  • Organizational Design
    Structural design and decision architecture for organizations building new systems or restructuring operations.

Brand & Growth Practice

  • Business Plan Development
    Structured business planning for founders and growth-stage brands—from market analysis and competitive positioning through financial modeling and go-to-market sequencing.
  • Marketing Strategy & Go-to-Market
    Strategic marketing plans and content systems grounded in brand meaning. Addresses channel strategy, audience architecture, and brand identity articulation.
  • Investor Readiness & Capital Preparation
    End-to-end preparation for capital conversations—from narrative development and pitch architecture through investor type mapping.
  • Custom Engagements
    High-stakes, bespoke advisory for unique organizational contexts or strategic initiatives requiring a tailored approach.

People & Performance Practice

  • Sales Training & Selling Skills
    Meaning-based approach to consultative selling for founders and sales teams. Particularly relevant for beauty and CPG brands selling into retail buyers, distributors, and channel partners.
  • Communication & Presentation Coaching
    High-stakes communication training for founders and leaders who need to own a room—pitches, board meetings, media, and investor conversations.
  • Talent & Team Development
    Hiring, onboarding, and retention frameworks grounded in meaning and culture fit. Builds scaling teams who carry the brand forward with integrity.
  • Culture Design
    Intentional culture-building from the ground up—values operationalization, norms, and rituals that hold as organizations grow.

How We Engage

Delivered as standalone engagements or as a sequenced suite—bundled or à la carte, in-person or virtual.

Standalone Engagements

Single workshops or advisory sessions scoped to a specific need. Ideal for brands testing the methodology or addressing one defined challenge before committing to a broader arc.

Retainer Relationships

Ongoing advisory partnerships providing consistent strategic support across organizational, brand, and people dimensions—as the business evolves and grows.

Bundled Suites

Sequenced combinations of workshops and advisory engagements designed to address a full strategic arc. Creates natural follow-on depth and compounds value across the relationship.
