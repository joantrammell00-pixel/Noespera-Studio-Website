The Founder

Dr. Joan deBien-Trammell brings 30+ years of strategic leadership experience across organizations including Gallup, L'Oréal, Johnson & Johnson, Pierre Fabre, and Sandoz Biopharmaceuticals.

As the founder of Organizational Meaning Science™, she has developed proprietary frameworks and methodologies that help boards, executives, and leadership teams make coherent decisions in complex, regulated, and high-stakes environments.

Her work translates organizational complexity into durable frameworks, decision architectures, and participatory methodologies grounded in rigorous research and applied across enterprise, nonprofit, and emerging organizational contexts.
